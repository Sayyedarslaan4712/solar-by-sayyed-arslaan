Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun, the third-largest planetary radius, and fourth-largest planetary mass in the Solar System.

Planet

Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun. In the Solar System, it is the fourth-largest planet by diameter and third-largest by mass.

Dwarf Planet

Pluto

In 2006, Pluto was reclassified as a dwarf planet. Known for its eccentric orbit, it sometimes comes closer to the sun than Neptune.
